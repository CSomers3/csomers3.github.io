Since the initiation of QE in the wake of the 2008 financial crisis, the Federal Reserve has wielded significant influence over both the US and global financial markets. The ebb and flow of the S&P500, in particular, appear to be tethered to the decisions and inclinations of Jerome Powell. I thought it might be an interesting idea to gauge the sentiment within the options market regarding the next meeting on Wednesday (31/01/24). To do this I will employ risk-neutral distributions inferred through options.
